About the company

Yota came to us to design an innovative and efficient way of talking to their customers.
The Yota app will ship pre-installed on their devices and will provide access to all services the company offers to their customers on the go,
from checking an account balance and data usage to constructing custom plans and chatting with support.

Features

We wanted to design an app that closed followed the Material Design guidelines, but at the same time belonged to the Yota family.
We decided to combine Yota’s iconic look with the bright, cheerful appearance of Android Lollipop.
At the time, Material Design had just been announced and we couldn’t wait to apply its novel and purposeful iconography,
animation, and interface design principles on a real project.
