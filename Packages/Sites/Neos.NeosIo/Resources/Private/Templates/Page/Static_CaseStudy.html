About the company

Yota came to us to design an innovative and efficient way of talking to their customers.
The Yota app will ship pre-installed on their devices and will provide access to all services the company offers to their customers on the go,
from checking an account balance and data usage to constructing custom plans and chatting with support.

Why choosing Neos made the difference?

Scaling content model

Neos scaled with our requirements as we proceeded. The finegrained content model made it possible to develop the site while still being in the process of conception and design.

Cloud ready

Since Neos is backed with the enterprise Flow PHP framework, it was a simple task to move the infrastructure into the cloud and scale it up to our needs.

Inline-Editing

The inline-editing made it possible to save important time and to avoid long and costly workshops for the editors. They just log into the backend and start editing right away!
