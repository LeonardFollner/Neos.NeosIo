What Neos can do for you...

What is Neos?

Think of Neos as an open source Content Application Platform based on it's own PHP framework Flow.
The Content Management features are resting within a larger context that allows you to build a perfectly customized experience.

Easily extendable

Requirements will change during the lifecycle of your project. This is why we put a lot of effort into making Neos as flexible as possible, while presenting a purpose-built, clean interface.

Built to be Customized

The most basic way of editing content in Neos is available by just clicking what you want to change, working out the changes and pressing the publish button. It's that simple.

Find out more about Neos now!

Admin Interface

Neos gives you a beautiful, interactive, customisable Admin UI.

Ready for the Cloud

Since Flow is cloud-ready, so is Neos. Neos scales with your business and your demands.
You plan an intense marketing campaign? Simply add as many servers as needed to your environment.

Neos supports modern deployment scenarios and works great with Docker, scalable clusters,
cloud storages and Content Delivery Networks.

Learn more about working with Neos in the Cloud

What you build is up to you.

There are a lot of content management systems that make decisions for you,
and many that take decisions away. Neos doesn't do that.

Use the features that suit you, and replace or adjust the ones that don't.

Learn more about extending Neos

Responsive Web

Since the advanced content editing mechanisms in Neos work together with your website you can change your contents directly within a responsive viewport – getting immediate results and reducing the time of testing your contents on different devices.

Learn more how to integrate your website in Neos
